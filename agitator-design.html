WHAT THIS DOES

Sizes a top-entry agitator for a vertical tank: impeller, mixing Reynolds number, motor power, torque, shaft diameter and baffles — for water-like liquids under standard conditions.

TANK & AGITATOR 🌀

Standard configuration: impeller D ≈ T/3, four wall baffles of width T/10. Power and shaft size follow from speed, density and the power number.
