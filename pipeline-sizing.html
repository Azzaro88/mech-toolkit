3 DARCY–WEISBACH 📉

ΔPf = f · (L/D) · (ρ V² / 2)
ΔPtotal = ΔPf + Σ K · (ρ V²/2) + ρ g Δz
hf = f · (L/D) · V²/(2 g)  ·  ΔP = ρ g hf
g = 9.81 m/s² · ΣK = sum of minor-loss coefficients · Δz = elevation change (m)

3.3 FRICTION FACTOR 🔣

Laminar (Re < 2300)
f = 64 / Re
Turbulent (Re ≥ 2300) — Colebrook–White (implicit)
1/√f = −2 log₁₀( ε/D / 3.7 + 2.51 / (Re·√f) )

4 SIZING PROCEDURE 🧭

  1. Specify inputs: Q, L, fluid props (ρ, μ or ν), ε, Δz, ΔPallow, Vmin, Vmax
  2. Assume diameter D (trial)
  3. Calculate V = 4Q/(πD²) · check Vmin ≤ V ≤ Vmax
  4. Calculate Re = ρVD/μ · determine flow regime & f
  5. Calculate ΔPtotal (Darcy + minor + elevation)
  6. Check & adjust: ΔPtotal ≤ ΔPallow → OK · else change D & repeat
Iterate until both pressure-drop and velocity criteria are satisfied.
