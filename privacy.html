1. Overview

MECH Toolkit ("we", "the site") provides free interactive engineering reference sheets and calculators. This policy explains what data is processed when you use the site and what choices you have.

2. Information we collect

  • Calculator inputs: all calculations run entirely in your browser. The numbers you type are not sent to us or stored on any server.
  • Local storage: we save your cookie-consent choice in your browser's local storage so we don't ask again on every visit.
  • Automatically collected data: like most websites, our hosting provider and any third-party services (below) may receive standard technical data such as your IP address, browser type, device and pages visited.

3. Cookies & similar technologies

A cookie is a small file stored on your device. We use them to remember your consent choice and, if you accept, to show advertising. You can manage or clear cookies in your browser settings at any time, and re-open our consent banner via the “Cookie settings” link in the footer.

4. Advertising — Google AdSense

If you accept cookies, we may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Learn more from Google's advertising policies and opt out of third-party vendors at aboutads.info / youronlinechoices.eu (EU).

If you decline cookies, advertising cookies are not loaded by this site.

5. Affiliate links

Some outbound links are affiliate links (for example, the Amazon Associates program). If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. We only link to products we consider relevant to engineers. Affiliate links do not influence the calculations or content.

6. Your choices

  • Click Decline on the consent banner to prevent advertising cookies from loading.
  • Adjust or delete cookies through your browser settings.
  • Manage Google ad personalisation via Google Ads Settings.

7. Children's privacy

This site is intended for professional and educational use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
