WHAT IS HEAT LOAD?

  • Total heat entering a conditioned space
  • From external + internal sources
  • Sized to hold the design indoor temperature

1.1 SECTIONS & SOURCES OF HEAT LOAD 🏠

EXTERNAL LOADS (enter from outside)
  1. Solar Radiation — through walls, windows, roof
  2. Heat Conduction — through walls, roof, floors, partitions
  3. Ventilation & Infiltration — outside air enters the building
INTERNAL LOADS (generated inside)
  1. Lighting — heat generated by lamps
  2. People — heat generated by occupants
  3. Appliances & Equipment — electrical devices
TOTAL HEAT LOAD = SUM OF ALL LOADS (to maintain the design indoor temperature)

1.2 METHOD ➗

Each source is calculated separately, then summed.

Qsolar+ Qtrans+ Qvent+ Qinternal= Qtotal
Qtotal = Qsolar + Qtrans + Qvent + Qinternal
Unit: Watt (W)
