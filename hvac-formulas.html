HOW TO USE

Each card is an independent live calculator — edit the inputs and the result updates instantly. SI units (L/s, W / kW, °C, Pa, m). Air ρ = 1.2 kg/m³.

1 AIRFLOW RATE (L/s) 🌀

V̇ = Q / (1.2 × ΔT)
Airflow

2 SENSIBLE HEAT (SH) ♨️

SH = 1.2 × V̇ × ΔT
SH

3 TOTAL HEAT (TH) 🔥

TH = SH + LH
TH

4 HEAT GAIN / LOSS 🌡️

Q = U × A × ΔT
Q (W)
Some sheets print “U + A×ΔT”; correct form is U × A × ΔT.

5 FAN POWER (kW) 🌬️

P = (V̇ × Δp) / (η × 10⁶)
Fan power
V̇ in m³/s × Δp in Pa = W; ÷1000 → kW.

6 AIR CHANGES PER HOUR 🏠

ACH = (V̇ × 3.6) / Volume
ACH (per hr)

7 SENSIBLE HEAT RATIO ⚖️

SHR = SH / TH
SHR

8 COEFF. OF PERFORMANCE ❄️

COP = Cooling / Power
COP
Both in kW → COP is dimensionless.

9 REFRIGERATION TON (TR) 🧊

TR = Cooling (kW) / 3.517
TR (tons)
1 TR = 3.517 kW = 12 000 Btu/hr.

10 DUCT FRICTION LOSS 🛠️

Δp = f × (L/D) × (ρ·V²/2)
Δp
Darcy–Weisbach with air ρ = 1.2 kg/m³ → Pa.

11 CHILLED WATER FLOW 💧

V̇w = Q / (4.186 × ΔT)
Flow
cp,water = 4.186 kJ/kg·K; 1 L ≈ 1 kg → L/s.

12 TOTAL COOLING FROM AIR 🖥️

Q = 1.2 × V̇ × Δh
Q

13 STATIC PRESSURE (SP) 🧭

SP = TP − VP
SP

14 PUMP HEAD (H) ⚙️

H = (P₂−P₁)/ρg + (Z₂−Z₁) + h_f
H (m)
Pressure head = ΔP(Pa)/(ρ·g), g = 9.81 m/s².

15 VENTILATION AIR (Qv) 👥

Qv = R_p × P_z
Qv (L/s)
