HOW TO USE

Each card is an independent live calculator — edit the inputs and the result updates instantly. Imperial units (CFM, Btu/hr, °F, in.wg, ft).

1 AIRFLOW RATE (CFM) 🌀

CFM = Q / (1.08 × ΔT)
CFM

2 SENSIBLE HEAT (SH) ♨️

SH = 1.08 × CFM × ΔT
SH (Btu/hr)

3 TOTAL HEAT (TH) 🔥

TH = SH + LH
TH (Btu/hr)

4 HEAT GAIN / LOSS 🌡️

Q = U × A × ΔT
Q (Btu/hr)
Sheet prints “U + A×ΔT”; correct form is U × A × ΔT.

5 FAN POWER (HP) 🌬️

HP = (CFM × SP) / (6356 × η)
HP

6 AIR CHANGES PER HOUR 🏠

ACH = (CFM × 60) / Volume
ACH (per hr)

7 SENSIBLE HEAT RATIO ⚖️

SHR = SH / TH
SHR

8 COEFF. OF PERFORMANCE ❄️

COP = Cooling / Power
COP
Btu/hr → W (÷3.412) so COP is dimensionless.

9 REFRIGERATION TON (TR) 🧊

TR = Cooling (Btu/hr) / 12000
TR (tons)

10 DUCT FRICTION LOSS 🛠️

FL = (4 × f × L × V²) / (2 × D)
FL (per sheet)
Sheet’s simplified form. Real Darcy: f·(L/D)·V²/2g with V in ft/s.

11 CHILLED WATER FLOW 💧

GPM = Q / (500 × ΔT)
Flow (GPM)

12 COOLING FROM AIRFLOW 🖥️

Q = 4.5 × CFM × ΔT
Q (Btu/hr)

13 STATIC PRESSURE (SP) 🧭

SP = TP − VP
SP (in.wg)

14 PUMP HEAD (H) ⚙️

H = (P₂−P₁)/γ + (Z₂−Z₁) + h_f
H (ft)
Pressure head = (P₂−P₁)×144/γ (psi→psf).

15 VENTILATION AIR (Qv) 👥

Qv = R_p × P_z
Qv (CFM)
