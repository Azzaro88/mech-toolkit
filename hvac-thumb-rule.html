5 DUCTWORK THUMB RULE 🛠️

Velocity

  • Main Duct (Supply): 6 – 8 m/s
  • Branch Duct: 4 – 6 m/s
  • Final Duct / Diffuser: 2 – 4 m/s
  • Return Duct: 5 – 7 m/s
  • Exhaust Duct: 6 – 10 m/s
Size duct for velocity in range & pressure drop ≤ 1.0 Pa/m (external), ≤ 0.5 Pa/m (internal branch).

7 CHILLED WATER THUMB RULE ❄️

  • ΔT (Supply – Return): 5 – 7 °C  ·  Common: 7 °C
  • CHW Temp: 7 °C Supply / 12 – 14 °C Return
  • Chiller Capacity = Total Cooling Load / COP
  • COP ≈ 3.0 – 5.0 (water cooled) · 2.5 – 3.5 (air cooled)
  • Pump Head: Building 25 – 40 m · System 15 – 25 m
Chiller Capacity — Load / COP
CHW Flow — Q = kW / (4.18 × ΔT)

9 ELECTRICAL LOAD THUMB RULE ⚡

  • HVAC Electrical Load ≈ 1.0 – 1.5 W/ft²
  • Chiller Power ≈ Capacity (TR) × 0.8 – 1.2 kW/TR
  • Pump Power ≈ (Flow L/s × Head m) / (75 × η), η≈0.7
  • Fan Power ≈ (Airflow m³/s × SP Pa) / η, η≈0.6
  • Add 15 – 20 % for future / diversity
